Wondering which states are poly?

Polycarbonate is a material that higher security IDs are printed on. It is used to prevent tampering, as the layers of polycarbonate are fused together. Teslin is several thin paper layers glued together, which can be taken apart and tampered. Some states use Teslin and other states use Polycarbonate for their real IDs. If a state is offered in polycarbonate, it is the best option.
